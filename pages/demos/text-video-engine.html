High-resolution text video, driven by Pretext.

This demo uses a reusable @chenglou/pretext/video engine to plan obstacle-aware text layout and render the result to canvas at arbitrary output resolutions. Tune typography, pacing, line boxes, glow obstacles, multi-column flow, and export stills or recorded clips.

Capability notes
Why this is a video engine

Render at production scale

Export frames at 4K or 8K without changing the composition logic. Regions, obstacle paths, and line placement all scale from one declarative project.

Manual composition

Obstacle routing, reveal timing, and multi-region handoff are authored in userland with Pretext’s prepared text and cursor-based line stepping at the core.

Canvas-only paint

The engine plans frame geometry, then paints boxes, shadows, fills, and text directly to canvas. No DOM measurement loop is involved in the render path.
